# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability within this project, please follow the steps below:

1. **Do NOT open a public issue.** We will not respond to issues that are publicly disclosed. 
2. Email us at [your-email@example.com](mailto:your-email@example.com) with details on the issue.
3. Include any relevant information, such as the steps to reproduce the vulnerability, a detailed description, and any potential impact.

## Expected Response Time

We aim to respond to all security vulnerabilities within 48 hours. Depending on the severity, we will work on a resolution and notify you accordingly.

## Security Updates

We will keep our users informed about security-related updates on our [GitHub](https://github.com/ShivamGawade-XS/sentinel-x) repository and other channels as appropriate.
